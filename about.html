About Ministry

The Indian Post Office is a government-operated postal system in India. It provides various services such as mail delivery, speed post, savings schemes, and insurance services.

With the largest postal network in the world, it plays an important role in connecting people across urban and rural areas.

Our aim is to provide reliable, affordable, and efficient services to all citizens.
